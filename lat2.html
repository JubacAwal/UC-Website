Young Farmers Challenge - National Level Competition



The Department of Agriculture has recognized Harry Damogo Osboken, UC CBA Class 2017, as one of the six ‘Outstanding Youth Agribusiness Models’ during the 1st National Young Farmers Summit and Young Farmers Challenge: Kabataang Agribiz Awarding Ceremony held on December 17, 2021, in Calasio, Pangasinan.

Mr. Osboken has received a cash grant amounting to PHP300 000 for his innovative agribusiness, Dalikan Rabbit Meat and Chili Processing, in the Cordillera Administrative Region.
